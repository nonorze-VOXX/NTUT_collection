Gameplay

Among Us is a multiplayer game for four to fifteen players (previously four to ten players), although recommended for at least five.[13] Up to three players are randomly[14] and secretly chosen to be the Impostor(s) each round. As of 2021, a round can take place on one of four maps: a spaceship called "The Skeld"; a headquarters building called "MIRA HQ"; a planet base called "Polus";[15] or an airship based on Innersloth's Henry Stickmin series called "The Airship".
